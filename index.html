      The orientation tax
    
    
AI assistants start every session blind.

    
They open without your branch, your running services, or your last checkpoint, so the conversation begins with interrogation, not work. Stale context isn't neutral. It's a tax on every session, and it compounds with every developer.

      How it works
    
    
Write directives once. Perseus resolves them to live facts every time.

    

      

        
1
Author

        
Write directives once in a markdown source file.

        
# .perseus/context.md
Branch: @query"git branch"
Services: @services
Tests: @query"pytest -q"

        
      

      

        
2
Render

        
Resolves directives in parallel, with caching: 31ms typical.
