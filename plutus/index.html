What it does

Every provider's spend in one ledger.

Meter every call

One track() per call writes an immutable, priced usage event: exact cost when you have it, estimated from tokens when you don't.

Prepaid-credit ledger

An append-only ledger depletes prepaid credit as calls land. Normalized across providers into a single local record you can query, export, and audit.

Live dashboard & alerts

A dark-themed dashboard shows balance, burn, and per-provider, workspace, and task breakdowns. Low-balance and budget-cap alerts warn you early.

One command

One track() per call. Prepaid credit, live.

Meter from the CLI, or drop one track() into your agent's hot path. Perseus can pull the remaining prepaid credit into your context file so your assistant knows the budget it's working against.

plutus meter
$ plutus meter --provider anthropic --model claude-opus-4-8 \
             --task code_review --input 8200 --output 2400

✓ metered   $0.184   anthropic/claude-opus-4-8
✓ prepaid credit   $49.82 remaining

dashboard → http://localhost:8420 · local ledger
